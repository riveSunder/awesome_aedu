Platforms

  • Complexity Explorer from the Santa Fe Institute contains ALife-related content including courses on:
    • Origins of Life
    • Epistemological Emergence
    • Multicellularity Modules

Courses

  • CS 361: Artificial Life and Digital Evolution taught by Anya Vostinar at Carleton College. Winter 2021: syllabus, schedule; Fall 2022: syllabus, schedule; and Spring 2025: syllabus, schedule.
  • Ludobots online course on evolutionary simulated robotics using reddit by Josh Bongard at the University of Vermont. Now with a differentiable programming version: diffdesign.
  • Evolutionary Computation by Dennis G. Wilson at the University of Toulouse, with course repo.
  • VassiLife course by Vassilis Papadopoulos at EPFL.
  • Introduction to Artificial Life by Mizuki Oka as part of University of Tsukuba OpenCourseWare (Japanese Language).
